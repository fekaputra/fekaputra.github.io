Welcome!

Hi there! I am Fajar, currently working as an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Data, Process, and Knowledge Management (DPKM) WU Wien and as a (part-time) Postdoctoral researcher at the Data Science research unit TU Wien.

My research interest is mainly in the Semantic Web, Knowledge (Graphs) Engineering, and their interaction with Machine Learning approaches within Neurosymbolic AI context. I am also interested in applying such hybrid systems in various application domains, including Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) and materials engineering.

I only updated this website every now and then; therefore please also check my webpage at WU Wien, and our Semantic System group website to get the latest news!
